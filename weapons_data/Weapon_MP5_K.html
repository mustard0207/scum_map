MP5 K

Weapon_MP5_K

The MP5K is a 9mm submachine gun, developed in the 1960s. The MP5K is one of the most widely used submachine guns in the world, having been adopted by 40 nations and numerous military, law enforcement, intelligence, and security organizations. This is a shorter version of the MP5 with no stock. It uses 9mm ammunition.
