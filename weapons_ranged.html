Each skin/variant as its own card

Ranged weapons—rifles, pistols, shotguns, SMGs, LMGs, bows, and more—are listed under Gear → Ranged Weapons (or Melee for bladed ranged where applicable). Each card and detail page surfaces damage, fire rate, range, caliber, attachments, and compatible magazines and ammunition where the pipeline provides them.

Use in-page subtabs (assault rifle, handgun, bow, and similar) and the right-hand filters for caliber, class, fire mode, and numeric minimums. Charms, ammo and magazines, attachments, and throwables live on their own Gear tabs.
